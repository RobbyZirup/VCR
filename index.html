FAQ - Casino Questions Answered

What are wagering requirements at online casinos?

Wagering requirements (also called playthrough) tell you how many times you must bet your bonus before withdrawing. For example, a $100 bonus at 35x means you must wager $3,500 total. A no wagering casino bonus skips this entirely - any winnings are instantly withdrawable. Always check this before claiming any welcome offer.

A casino no KYC (Know Your Customer) lets you play and withdraw without submitting identity documents like a passport or driver's licence. What is RTP in slots and why does it matter?

RTP (Return to Player) is the percentage a slot machine pays back over millions of spins. A slot with 96% RTP returns $96 per $100 wagered on average. The best slots RTP 2026 include Mega Joker (99%), Blood Suckers (98%), and Starmania (97.87%). Always play high-RTP slots -it makes a real difference to your long-term results.

Are online casinos rigged?

Licensed online casinos are not rigged. They use certified Random Number Generators (RNGs) audited by independent labs like eCOGRA, iTech Labs, and GLI. Every casino on our list holds a valid gambling licence and has passed third-party fairness testing. Never play at an unlicensed site - those are the ones to avoid.

What casino games have the best odds?

The games with the best odds (lowest house edge) are:
