How to Read Casino Bonus Terms

Know what you're claiming before you click

📋 Wagering Requirement

The number of times you must bet your bonus before withdrawing. 30x is fair, 50x+ is high. A $100 bonus at 35x = $3,500 total wagers needed.

🎲 Game Contribution

Not all games count equally toward wagering. Slots: 100%. Live casino: 10–20%. Some table games: 0%. Always check the T&C before playing.

💰 Max Bet Rule

Most casinos cap your bet at $5 per spin while a bonus is active. Placing higher bets can void your bonus and any winnings from it.

⌛ Bonus Expiry

Bonuses typically expire in 7–30 days. Free spins often expire in 24–72 hours. Always check the expiry window before claiming.

🚫 Max Cashout

Many bonuses cap winnings at $100–$500. No wagering bonuses often have no cap, letting you withdraw all winnings. Read the small print!

✅ No Wagering Bonus

A no wagering casino bonus has zero playthrough. Any winnings are yours instantly - no strings attached. Rarer but always worth prioritising.

Casino Bonus FAQ

What is a no wagering casino bonus?

A no wagering casino bonus means any winnings you earn from the bonus or free spins are yours to keep immediately - no playthrough required. These are the best bonuses for players because there are no hidden conditions attached to your winnings.

What is a casino welcome bonus no deposit?

A no deposit bonus gives you free spins or bonus chips just for creating an account — no payment needed. It is a risk-free way to test a casino. These bonuses usually have higher wagering requirements (40x–60x) and a lower max cashout (often $50–$100).

How do I read casino bonus terms and conditions?

Focus on five key points: (1) the wagering requirement (lower is better), (2) game contribution rates, (3) maximum bet per round while wagering (usually $5), (4) the bonus expiry window, and (5) any maximum cashout limit on bonus winnings.

Which casino has the best welcome bonus in 2026?

For overall value, VegasPlus (€2,250 + 100FS at 35x) and North Casino (CA$5,000 at 35x) lead on package size. For fairness, casinos with 30x or lower wagering requirements and no max cashout offer the best real value regardless of headline amount.

Can I withdraw casino bonus winnings immediately?

Only if you claimed a no wagering bonus. With standard bonuses, you must complete the wagering requirement first. Once met, most casinos process withdrawals within 24–72 hours. Make sure your KYC documents are verified in advance to avoid delays.
